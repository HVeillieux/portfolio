Bikes Galore!

The idea behind this project was to get people interested in loaning bikes from the Paterson Library at Confederation College. While the idea is still in the works, this poster scheme was a first step in the right direction. The posters were meant to showcase a bicycle with text detailing the loaning information. Hopefully, with a bit more tweaking these posters will be the start of something pretty cool.

Have a Tissue!

This was a simple design I did for a sticker that would be placed on tissue packages that could be handed out to students. It's a promotional nod to the AskON help center that the Paterson Library runs. We used the first version as the final print.

EarthBound

The APIWIN Centre at Confederation College was looking to start a new student newsletter. One in which anyone could submit art, poems, stories and more. I was asked to make up the logo. Unfortunately the zine was put on the sidelines for now, but I have enjoyed working on the possible logos and layouts for the magazine. *sidenote: the Ojibwe translation is super rough - I'll update the final version at a later date

Negahneewin Book Club

This quick project was a bookmark used to spread the word about the Neghaneewin Indigenous book club that features new authors monthly. I took inspiration from all sorts of places. But I wanted the tree of knowledge to be the main focus here. The roots, made up of a book, represent the knowledge we seek. This in turn feeds our minds, growing that very tree.
